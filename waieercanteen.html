The Waaier Canteen

Where Campus Life Comes Together

About the Venue

Located at the heart of the University of Twente campus, The Waaier Canteen serves as a dynamic hub for students, researchers, and faculty alike. Our modern dining hall isn't just a place to eat—it's a space designed for collaboration, relaxation, and high-quality nourishment.

We pride ourselves on offering an international organic cuisine that celebrates global flavors while respecting local sustainability. Every dish is crafted with precision to provide the energy needed for a rigorous academic day.

Heritage of
Innovation

Founded at the University of Twente in 1961, the Department of Business Studies & Tech Policy has been a cornerstone of the institution's "High Tech, Human Touch" philosophy. We bridge the gap between complex engineering advancements and the strategic frameworks required to navigate global markets and ethical governance.

Our focus remains steadfast on high-tech solutions that address contemporary societal challenges. By integrating rigorous economic analysis with deep technical understanding, we prepare our students to lead in an era defined by rapid digital transformation and evolving policy landscapes.

Our Vision

To be a world-leading hub for interdisciplinary research that redefines the relationship between technology and society, fostering a future where innovation serves as a catalyst for equitable global progress.

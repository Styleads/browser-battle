The Academic Heart of Campus

The Vrijhof Library stands as the University of Twente's central knowledge hub, meticulously designed to support the academic excellence of our "High Tech Human Touch" community.

We provide seamless access to vast digital and physical collections, ranging from specialized engineering journals to extensive humanities archives. Understanding the rigors of academic life, we offer 24/7 exam access during peak study periods, ensuring our students have the space they need to succeed when it matters most.

"A sanctuary for focused research and collaborative innovation."

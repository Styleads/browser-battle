# browser-battle
A modern, immersive website for **University of Twente** (Netherlands):
https://styleads.github.io/browser-battle/index.html ✨ Project Overview

We created a premium digital experience for University of Twente — one of Europe's innovative technical universities known for its green campus, strong engineering & technology programs, and excellent employability.

The website focuses on helping prospective international students (especially from India) easily explore programs, understand admissions, and estimate their chances.

## 🎯 Features Built till now:

### Completed Sections
- **Homepage** — Stunning hero with campus visuals, key highlights, and smooth animations
- **Academic Programs** — Clean, attractive cards showcasing Bachelor's and Master's programs
- **Admissions Page** — Comprehensive and interactive:
  - Clear Bachelor's & Master's admission requirements (tailored for Indian students)
  - Step-by-step application process timeline
  - Required documents list
  - Tuition fees & estimated living costs for 2026-2027
  - Scholarships information (UTS & NL Scholarship)
  - **Interactive Admission Probability Estimator** — Users input their scores and instantly see their estimated chance + personalized tips
 
    ***Admission Requirements***(Indian Students)

Bachelor’s: Minimum 75% in Class 12th (with strong Mathematics for technical programs)
Master’s: 4-year Bachelor’s degree with minimum 75% CGPA (First Class). IIT/NIT students may get slight relaxation in some cases.
English Proficiency: IELTS 6.0–6.5 (Bachelor’s) | 6.5 (Master’s) | Equivalent TOEFL scores

Tuition Fees (Non-EU students, 2026-2027)


***Bachelor’s***: Approx. €12,300 – €16,400 per year
***Master’s***: €18,200 (alpha/gamma programs) – €21,700 (beta/technical programs)

**Important Deadlines**   



Main application deadline for non-EU students (Sept 2026 intake): 1 May 2026
University of Twente Scholarship (UTS) Rounds: 1 Feb 2026 & 1 May 2026

***Scholarships***   



University of Twente Scholarship (UTS): €3,000 – €22,000 per year (merit-based, ~50 awarded annually)
NL Scholarship: €5,000 (one-time for first year)

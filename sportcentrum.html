Elite Training Ground for the Digital Polymath

At the University of Twente, we believe physical excellence is the perfect partner to academic rigor. The Sportcentrum UT is a state-of-the-art hub designed to cater to everyone—from recreational hobbyists to elite student-athletes.

With facilities for over 40 sports, including specialized equipment for swimming and a world-class indoor climbing wall, we provide the infrastructure needed to push your limits.

"High tech meets high performance. The facilities here are among the best in the Netherlands."

— Director of Campus Sports

Research Excellence

NanoLab Twente

World-Class Nanotechnology Research

MESA+ Institute for Nanotechnology

Pioneering at the
Atomic Scale

NanoLab Twente offers a unique ecosystem for high-tech research and development. Spanning over 1,250 square meters of state-of-the-art infrastructure, our facility serves as the beating heart of the MESA+ Institute.

Designed for precision, the lab provides an environment where researchers can manipulate matter at the molecular level, enabling breakthroughs in semiconductor technology, micro-electromechanical systems (MEMS), and quantum computing.
